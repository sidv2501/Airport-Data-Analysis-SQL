# ✈️ Airport Passenger Traffic Analysis using SQL

## Project Overview

This project analyzes airport passenger traffic using SQL. Various analytical queries were written to understand passenger movement, busiest routes, airport performance, seat utilization, and the relationship between city population and airport traffic.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze passenger traffic across routes
- Find busiest and least busy routes
- Calculate average passengers per flight
- Measure airport traffic
- Identify high traffic corridors
- Analyze airline seat utilization
- Find top destination airports
- Compare city population with airport traffic

---

## SQL Analysis Performed

### 1. Total Passenger Traffic

Analyzed passenger movement by year, quarter, origin city and destination city.

---

### 2. Top 5 Busiest Routes

Identified routes with the highest passenger traffic.

---

### 3. Least Busy Routes

Found routes having the minimum passenger count.

---

### 4. Average Passengers

- Average passengers per route
- Average passengers per airport

---

### 5. Flight Frequency

Calculated number of flights between cities.

---

### 6. Seat Utilization

Estimated seat utilization percentage for each airline.

---

### 7. Popular Destination Airports

Ranked airports based on inbound passengers.

---

### 8. City Population vs Passenger Traffic

Compared city population with total passenger traffic.
